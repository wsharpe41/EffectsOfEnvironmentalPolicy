United Nations Convention to Combat Desertification Background

The United Nations Convention to Combat Desertification was held in 1994 with the purpose of reducing drought and the effects of drought. It has been almost universally accepted globally since 1994. This convention promoted the formation of national as well as regional programmes across the globe with a shared goal. Along with the programs a group of experts was established to publicly disseminate best practices and current knowledge regarding desertification.
